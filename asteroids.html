Instructions

The main goal of this game is destroy all the asteroids as fast as possible. Destroy more asteroids in order to get a higher score.

On this Dapp Demo is not important the points you get. The most important thing is that you can take a little bit of our future work and enjoy time with it.

Support us. As soon as possible, we expect to release many games for all liskers!

Controls

1. Move the spaceship with WASD keys.

2. Shot with left key of the mouse.

3. Change orientation with mouse.
