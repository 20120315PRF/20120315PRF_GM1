Instructions

Aliens are trying to invade us. You are the only one who can help the humanity to avoid his attack!

Destroy all enemy spaceships. In order to protect the Earth, you must shoot all of them and get a high score!

Levels are designed to increase the difficulty. Be careful out there!

Controls

1. Move the spaceship with WASD keys or with arrows.

2. Shot with spacebar.

3. Enjoy.
